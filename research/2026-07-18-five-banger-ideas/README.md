## Selection logic

This session intentionally widens the aperture after the first deep package. The goal is not to pick five random startups. The goal is to identify five directions with enough technical uncertainty, institutional depth, and alignment with Max's world to deserve future deep research.

## One sentence thesis

The strongest unexplored directions are not more chatbots; they are systems that make AI action governable: by owning data lineage, rehearsing actions, preserving creative provenance, encoding operational boundaries, and storing reusable autonomy scenarios.

## Main insight

Cadence is not just a project. It is a working pattern: human-gated, provenance-preserving, private, auditable AI operations. The five ideas below are different ways of turning that pattern into infrastructure.

## Recommended next action

Deepen **Cadence — Sovereign Training Data Foundry** first, because it is already partially real. Then use the other four ideas as adjacent research branches that can feed its architecture: evaluation, provenance, ops, and autonomy scenarios.

## Unexpected Connection

The strange connection is that Cadence, C2PA, aviation simulators, and agent dry-runs all point at the same primitive: **proof-carrying action**. A system should not merely perform work; it should carry evidence that the work was authorized, reversible where possible, evaluated before execution, and traceable afterward.
