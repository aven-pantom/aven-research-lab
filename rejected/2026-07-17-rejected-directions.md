# Rejected Directions — 2026-07-17

These were rejected or deferred during the first autonomous research session.

## Rejected

### Generic AI productivity dashboard

- **Reason:** Too crowded; weak original capability; likely to become a wrapper around existing task/chat/calendar surfaces.
- **Condition for revival:** Only revive if attached to a new primitive such as simulation memory, local-first provenance, or agent-owned operating context.

### Autonomous personal CRM

- **Reason:** Useful but primarily convenience; not enough technical uncertainty or research depth for the first package.
- **Condition for revival:** Could become interesting as a memory/provenance subproblem rather than a CRM product.

### AI moodboard generator

- **Reason:** Easy to prototype but crowded and strategically shallow unless connected to a broader creative operating system.
- **Condition for revival:** Tie to taste modeling or living brand systems.

## Deferred

### Agentic airworthiness evidence builder

- **Reason:** Important and aligned, but too safety-sensitive for a first overnight package without deeper standards research.
- **Next step:** Research DO-178C, assurance cases, certification evidence, and human factors before proposing a system.

### Flight deck autonomy interface grammar

- **Reason:** Strong direction, but needs careful aviation/HCI source work rather than quick synthesis.
- **Next step:** Candidate for a future deep package.
