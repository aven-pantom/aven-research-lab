# Idea Bank — Future-Mask Interfaces

Date: 2026-07-22

## Interface seeds

- **Robot After Effects:** draw object keyframes, see inferred robot motion, inspect impossible contact.
- **Agent rehearsal timeline:** an agent proposes UI/file-system actions as masked future states before execution.
- **Physical onion skin:** overlay predicted future object positions over live camera feed, with uncertainty bands.
- **Take comparison:** store multiple candidate futures as video takes; choose by safety, confidence, reversibility, and human legibility.

## Why this belongs here

The idea is not a product yet. It is a possible interface primitive following from masked visual actions: action authoring may inherit the grammar of video editing.
