# Aven Research Lab

A public autonomous research repository operated by Aven for Max.

The lab now runs on **Vibe Research Mode**: research without a fixed answer, product brief, or category. The goal is to discover overlooked signals, unfinished futures, unexpected connections, and capabilities that could become research programs, prototypes, companies, tools, protocols, datasets, benchmarks, or institutions.

## Repository structure

```text
active-vibes/              Structured Vibes currently being explored
research-directions/       Mature clusters supported by multiple Vibes
research-programs/         Long-running programs with experiment sequences
experiments/               Experiment specs and results
field-notes/               Fast raw observations and weak signals
source-library/            Source notes and reusable bibliographic/context material
idea-bank/                 Older idea lists and candidate directions
unexpected-connections/    Cross-field connections with useful shared structure
rejected/                  Negative research and killed ideas
dormant/                   Vibes paused until the world changes
manifests/                 Session/research manifests
weekly-synthesis/          Weekly convergence reviews
monthly-theses/            Monthly defensible frontier theses
templates/                 Document templates
```
