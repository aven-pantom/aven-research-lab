# Five Banger Directions — 2026-07-18

These are the five high-potential directions selected after the initial Simulation Memory package. This is an exploratory portfolio, not a claim of completed experiments.

| Rank | Idea | Field | Why it matters | Potential | Prototype difficulty | Status |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Cadence — Sovereign Training Data Foundry | Private AI datasets, provenance, training ops | Turns human-gated dataset governance into reusable infrastructure | Very high | Medium | Highest-priority applied direction |
| 2 | Rehearsal Bench for Tool-Using Agents | Agent evaluation, simulation, devtools | Tests whether agents can safely rehearse before real actions | Very high | Medium-high | Strong research direction |
| 3 | Provenance-Native Creative Engine | Creative AI, C2PA, design systems | Makes AI creative output inspectable and defensible | High | Medium | Product/research hybrid |
| 4 | Runbook-Aware Ops Agent | AI ops, infrastructure automation | Encodes bounded agency for infrastructure work | High | Medium | Practical company wedge |
| 5 | Scenario Bank for Autonomous Interfaces | Robotics, aviation, HCI | Preserves reusable situations for autonomy handoff/recovery | Very high | High | Long-horizon research |

## Recommended priority

1. Deepen Cadence into a product architecture.
2. Prototype Rehearsal Bench as a benchmark/evaluation layer.
3. Use Provenance-Native Creative Engine as a Pantom-aligned product experiment.
4. Extract Runbook-Aware Ops Agent from Aven/Cadence operations.
5. Research Scenario Bank slowly with aviation/robotics sources before building.

## Why Cadence is first

Cadence is the only one already backed by operational reality. It has private data custody, human review, immutable dataset versions, validation, and bounded execution gates. That makes it a stronger immediate research/company candidate than ideas that exist only as concepts.
