# Idea Bank — New Camera Apps That Capture Something Else

Date: 2026-07-19
Related Vibe: [Cameras Should Capture Change, Not Pictures](../active-vibes/cameras-should-capture-change-not-pictures.md)

These are deliberately not feature variants of Instagram/Snap/Lightroom. Each one changes the capture target.

## Strongest initial product shape

Start with **Atmosphere Shutter + Delta Camera + Creative Reference Camera**.

Why:

- They are legible in one tap.
- They do not require always-on surveillance.
- They produce artifacts creatives actually want.
- They can be prototyped with existing phone media and local models.
- Together they define a new category: camera as field instrument, not social feed input.

## Interface principles

- The shutter should ask for intent only after capture when possible; moments move faster than menus.
- Modes should feel like verbs: `notice`, `compare`, `trace`, `prove`, `remember`.
- The artifact should be beautiful enough to keep, but structured enough to search.
- Raw capture should remain private by default; shareable exports should be deliberate reductions.
- The app should preserve texture: exact light, sound, timing, uncertainty, and odd details — not flatten everything into generic summaries.
