# Source Notes — Printable Quiet Persistent Aircraft

## Integrity note

This source note was created from programmatic metadata searches through arXiv, Crossref, and Semantic Scholar on 2026-07-19. It preserves only claims supported by retrieved metadata/abstract text. It does not claim aircraft construction, performance, range, acoustic level, autonomy capability, or operational suitability.

## Safety boundary

The source map is intentionally non-operational. It avoids build dimensions, material recipes, flight profiles, payload integration, stealth tactics, or surveillance tactics. The research object is the system-level possibility of printable airframe grammars, quiet-electric constraints, civil remote-sensing legitimacy, and accountability infrastructure.
